# Gig Calculator - Comprehensive Profitability Calculator

A professional calculator for freelancers and gig workers to calculate true net income per hour, including all business costs.

##  Features

- **Comprehensive Cost Tracking**
  - Vehicle costs per kilometer (fuel, insurance, maintenance, depreciation, tires)
  - Fixed monthly costs distributed per working hour
  - Equipment and supply costs per gig
  - YEL payment (entrepreneur's pension insurance)
  - Income tax calculations

- **Real-time Calculations**
  - Automatic updates as you type
  - Cost breakdown per working hour
  - Net income with color-coded indicators

- **Professional UI**
  - Dark theme with gradient accents
  - Fully responsive design
  - Mobile-friendly interface

## Use Cases

Perfect for:

- Gig economy workers
- Small business owners

##  How It Works

The calculator takes your hourly rate and distributes all your business costs proportionally across your working hours, giving you a realistic view of your actual net income.

##  Getting Started

Simply open the calculator and enter:
1. Your working hours and hourly rate
2. Distance traveled
3. Your actual vehicle costs
4. Monthly fixed costs
5. Tax rates

The calculator will show you:
- Total invoice amount (with VAT)
- Detailed cost breakdown
- Net income per hour
- Total take-home pay

##  Cost Breakdown Includes

- **Variable Costs:** Fuel, vehicle wear, equipment
- **Fixed Costs:** Accounting, rent, subscriptions, insurance, marketing
- **Taxes:** YEL payment, income tax
- **VAT:** Automatically calculated
